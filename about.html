About Me

Hi There!

I’m Ken Ashley Cabrales, a UX enthusiast with strong critical thinking skills and a sharp eye for design and program details. While I don't come from a traditional graphic design background, I've developed a solid understanding of design concepts and user experience through self-study, hands-on practice, and passion-driven experimentation. I love analyzing how people interact with interfaces and finding ways to make digital experiences more seamless and enjoyable. Outside of tech, I value creativity, collaboration, and staying curious about how things work.

My professional aspiration is to become a full-stack developer and UX designer who builds meaningful, user-centered applications that solve real problems. I’m constantly improving my skills in both front-end and back-end technologies while embracing design systems and accessibility best practices. I aim to work on projects that push boundaries and deliver impact—whether in startups or larger tech teams—where I can grow, contribute, and continue learning from industry leaders.
